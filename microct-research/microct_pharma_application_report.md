**Window:** Jan 2025 – Jun 2026  ·  **Generated:** 2026-06-24

## Gates (this run)
- **Application gate (replaces dosage form):** the CT/OCT is applied to something that **goes into a drug** (API, excipient, intermediate, blend, coating material) **or into the body** (an administered dosage form). Excludes devices/hardware, packaging, equipment, pure tissue/diagnostic imaging.
- **Method gate:** micro-CT / synchrotron µCT / X-ray microscopy (XRM) / nano-CT / XRD-CT **+ OCT**. (Not terahertz, SEM-only, planar X-ray, or MRI.)
- **Affiliation gate:** ≥1 pharma/biotech **drug-developer** author.
- **Hard exclusion:** anything with an **imaging-services / analytical-CRO / instrument-vendor** author (DigiM, Xploraytion, Novitom, Zeiss, Bruker, Rigaku, ELDICO, Finden, Nanopharm/Aptar, Thermo Fisher) **or DigiM** is removed entirely — not shown anywhere, including below.
- Two label columns: **CT method** and **Application** (into a drug / into the body).

## Coverage caveat
Proxy blocked publisher full text — every entry corroborated across ≥2 independent web-search snippets (plus author/company pages). Confirm DOIs before citing. Not an exhaustive PubMed/Scopus export. All company sweeps complete (incl. Bayer).
